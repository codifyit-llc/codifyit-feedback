# Welcome to CodifyIT Feedback

This repo is the public feedback front door for CodifyIT.

Use this space to share:

- ideas worth evaluating
- product improvements
- enhancement requests
- cross-product feedback

Before you post, read:

- `What Belongs Here`
- `What Not to Post Here`
- `How Decisions and Routing Work`

We keep replies visible and simple so you can understand what happens next
without needing to know CodifyIT's internal governance language.
