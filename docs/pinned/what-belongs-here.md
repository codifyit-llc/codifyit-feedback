# What Belongs Here

Post here when you want to share:

- an idea for a CodifyIT product or experience
- a meaningful improvement request
- a pattern you keep seeing across products
- feedback about how a product or workflow lands from the outside

Good posts usually explain:

- what you are seeing
- why it matters
- what better looks like
- who is affected

This repo works best when feedback stays focused on the problem or opportunity,
not on prescribing every implementation detail.
